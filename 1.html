• Biography of hackers Anonimus

        Anonimous as a group was founded in 2003 . The initial term of recognition in the early 2000s on the 4chan image board as a concept of numerous shared Internet users, making up one anarchic digital global brain . Their regulars called themselves anonymous, which is why participants automatically signed up if they did not specifically indicate their name. In the mid-2000s, young Canadian programmer Aubrey Cottle was one of the leaders of the image boards Something Awful and 4chan. In 2007, Cottle was contacted by a representative of the Canadian Security and Intelligence Service and offered to take part in the fight against international terrorists, including al-Qaeda.



        Anonymous's appeal to Putin

        In connection with the current events in Ukraine - the invasion of the Russian army on the territory of an independent state, waging a brutal war against civilians, destroying urban infrastructure, the hacker group Anonimous joins the global condemnation of the aggressor's actions.



        The international hacking group Anonymous has declared a "cyber war" against Russian authorities and Russian President Putin.

        "The Anonymous team is officially waging a cyber war against the Russian government ... Anonymous is currently involved in operations against Russia. Our operations are directed against the Russian government. Inevitably, the private sector is likely to be disrupted as well," the statement said.

        The group said it wanted the Russian people to understand that everyone knew "how difficult it is for them to oppose their dictator for fear of reprisals.".
